AMERICAN RAG

Los Angeles, United States

Brands to look out for:

BLK DNM, Canada Goose, Chapter, Comme des Garçons Play, Fiorentini + Baker, John Elliott + Co, LD Tuttle, Levi's Vintage, NSF, Obey, PRPS, Publish, Senso, Stampd, Swedish Hasbeens, Unif, Vivienne Westwood, Wildfox.

About the store:

Starting with shipping a few containers of vintage clothing from the French port of Marseille over 30 years ago, American Rag has become an institution and mainstay of Californian fashion and culture. The boutique’s founder, Mark Werts, and his team travel the world in pursuit of unique and exclusive pieces to offer in its Los Angeles and Newport Beach locations. Every piece is carefully selected to reflect American Rag’s dedication to heritage, style, elegance and, above all, quality.

ANTONIA MACAU

Macau , Macau SAR

Brands to look out for:

Buscemi, Giuseppe Zanotti Design, Gucci, Kenzo, Marcelo Burlon, Nike, Off White, Rick Owens, Saint Laurent, Thom Browne, Valentino, Versace

About the store:

One of the largest boutiques in Macau, Antonia is named after one half of its founding couple. A designer store that originated in Milan, its buyers have an eye for combining established designers with emerging names to provide customers with a personally curated selection. Sprawled across two levels, it is the first Antonia boutique in the Asia-Pacific region. The space is based on an avant-garde design concept whereby architect Vincenzo de Cotiis has created a unique shopping ambiance.
